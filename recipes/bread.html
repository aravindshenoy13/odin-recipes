Homemade White Bread Recipe

Description

This is the recipe to freshly made homemade white bread you can make for today's lunch!

Ingredients

  • Warm Water: 1 cup (110°F/45°C) to activate the yeast.
  • Sugar: 3 tablespoons to help the yeast ferment.
  • Vegetable Oil: 3 tablespoons for moisture and texture.
  • Salt: 1 ½ teaspoons for seasoning.
  • Bread Flour: 3 cups to give structure and texture to the dough.
  • Active Dry Yeast: 2 ¼ teaspoons to help the dough rise.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients: water, sugar, oil, salt, bread flour, and yeast.
  2. Place ingredients into the bread machine pan (or as per the manufacturer's instructions).
  3. Bake on the White Bread setting.
  4. Carefully remove the bread pan using oven mitts once baking is complete.
  5. Remove the bread from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.
